LEAVEWINDOW_CB

Action generated when the mouse leaves a canvas or button.

Callback

int function(Ihandle *self); [in C]
elem:leavewindow_cb() -> (ret: number) [in Lua]

self: identifier of the control from where the mouse left

Affects

IupCanvas, IupButton, IupGLCanvas

In Motif can also be used by other controls, but it is not available in Lua for those controls.
